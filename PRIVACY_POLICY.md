# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: December 22, 2024**

This Privacy Policy describes how Improvify ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## Summary

- We collect only what's necessary to provide the service
- Your journal entries are private and encrypted
- We don't sell your data
- You can delete your account and data anytime

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

| Data Type | Purpose | Required |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Email address | Account creation & login | Yes |
| Display name | Personalization | Optional |
| Profile photo | Profile customization | Optional |
| Journal entries | Core app functionality | Yes (to use features) |

### Information Generated Through Use

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Challenge completion data | Track progress, calculate XP and levels |
| Streak data | Daily streak tracking and shields |
| Life balance scores | Wheel of Life visualization (8 categories) |
| Theme preferences | Personalize your experience |
| Notification settings | Send reminders if enabled |

### Automatically Collected

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Device type & OS version | App compatibility and debugging |
| App crash reports | Fix bugs and improve stability |
| Anonymous usage patterns | Understand which features are valuable |

We do **not** collect: location data, contacts, browsing history, or data from other apps.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality
- Store and sync your journal entries across devices
- Generate personalized AI challenges from your reflections
- Calculate your progress (XP, levels, streaks, life balance)
- Send daily reminders and weekly insights (if enabled)

### Service Improvement
- Analyze anonymous usage patterns to improve features
- Debug crashes and performance issues
- Respond to support requests

### We Never
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your journal content for advertising
- Share your reflections with other users
- Train AI models on your personal content

---

## 3. AI Processing

When you save a reflection, the text is sent to **Deepseek** (our AI provider) to generate personalized challenges.

**Important:**
- Your reflection content is sent to Deepseek's API servers for processing
- Content is processed in real-time and **not permanently stored** by Deepseek
- Generated challenges are stored in your Improvify account
- AI categorizes challenges into life areas (Health, Career, Finance, Relationships, Personal Growth, Fun, Environment, Spirituality)
- We do not use your reflections to train AI models

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

| Measure | Implementation |
|---------|----------------|
| Database | Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security |
| Encryption in transit | TLS 1.3 for all API communications |
| Encryption at rest | AES-256 encryption for stored data |
| Authentication | Secure token storage using device keychain |
| Access control | Your data is isolated and only accessible by you |

Your journal entries are private. Even our team cannot read your personal reflections.

## 6. Your Rights & Choices

### You Can Always

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Access your data | View all your reflections and challenges in the app |
| Edit your profile | Settings → Edit Profile |
| Delete specific entries | Swipe to delete any reflection or challenge |
| Export your data | Contact us for a full data export |
| Delete your account | Settings → Delete Account (permanent, within 30 days) |
| Disable notifications | Settings → Notifications or device settings |

### Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained as long as account exists
- Deleted accounts: All data permanently removed within 30 days
- Anonymous analytics: May be retained indefinitely (not linked to you)
